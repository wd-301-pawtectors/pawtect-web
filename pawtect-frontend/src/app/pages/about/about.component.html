Who We Are

Pawtect is dedicated to protecting and improving the lives of stray, neglected, and rescued animals in Angeles City. Our mission is to bridge the gap between these vulnerable animals and compassionate individuals willing to adopt, support, and advocate for their welfare. Through a digital platform, we aim to increase adoption rates, provide resources for responsible pet ownership, and foster a strong community of animal advocates. By leveraging technology, education, and community engagement, Pawtect strives to create a future where every animal receives the care, love, and respect it deserves.

Our Goals & Advocacy

Mission

Our mission is to create a sustainable and inclusive platform that empowers individuals and organizations to improve the lives of animals in need.

Vision

We envision a future where every stray animal has a loving home and every community is engaged in animal welfare. By leveraging technology and community-driven efforts, we aspire to create a compassionate and well-informed society that prioritizes the protection, care, and rights of animals.
